🔒 BallroomBuddy is designed to work entirely on your device. Your audio is never transmitted to any server.

Overview

BallroomBuddy is a dance music identification app that uses artificial intelligence to suggest ballroom dance styles based on audio captured from your device microphone. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

What We Do NOT Collect

BallroomBuddy does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Account information (no accounts are required)
  • Payment information (processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play)

What Stays On Your Device

When you use BallroomBuddy:

  • The microphone captures 5 seconds of audio
  • Audio is processed locally on your device using on-device machine learning
  • The audio and all extracted features are discarded immediately after classification
  • Nothing is stored to disk beyond your app settings

App Settings

BallroomBuddy stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

  • Theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Display preference (top 3 results or all 9)
  • First launch flag (to show the welcome screen once)

These preferences are stored using your device's local storage and never leave your device.

Microphone Access

BallroomBuddy requests access to your device microphone solely to capture audio for dance style identification. Microphone access is only active during the 5-second recording window when you tap the LISTEN button.

BallroomBuddy uses your device's audio session in measurement mode — a setting that disables voice processing effects (noise cancellation, automatic gain control) to capture clean music audio. This is a local device configuration only and does not affect any data transmission.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device Settings.

Optional Feedback

BallroomBuddy includes an optional feedback form (via Google Forms) accessible from the app menu. This form is entirely voluntary.

If you choose to submit feedback:

  • Your responses are stored in Google Forms on servers operated by Google LLC
  • An email address is optional and only requested if you want a reply
  • Feedback data is used solely to improve the app
  • We will never sell, share, or use your feedback for any other purpose
  • Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

BallroomBuddy offers an optional support purchase ("Support BallroomBuddy"). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. BallroomBuddy does not receive, process, or store any payment information.

In-app purchases are consumable voluntary tips, not required to use any feature of the app.
